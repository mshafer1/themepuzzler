Welcome!

This site was built to help you practice with musical themes.

How to play

  • Select a piece from the menu
  • Listen to each part (A through the end)
  • Drag the parts to where you think they should be.
  • Repeat (maybe even play-all to hear your ordering) untill you think you have it right
  • Check your score.
  • Compete with your friends! (you can share the link the page gives you to have them get the exact same version).
